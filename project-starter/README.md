Analyzing Relationships between Microbial Communities in the Serengeti and the Environment
================
by AnaBelle Schwarze

## Introduction

The data used in this project comes from a study done in Serengeti National Park in Tanzania. This experiment involved the observation of bacterial and archaeal communities in the soil after the removal of mammalian herbivores for 13 years.
